About

Myself

A self-taught UI | UX Designer who is passionate about designing digital experiences that leave a positive impact on people, the environment, and society. Shortly after I finished school - I've made a conscious decision of moving into design, since then, I've been developing my skills in visual design and in UX. During the past 4-5 years of working in diverse industries including real estate, SaaS, and technology, with environments ranging from small startups to large-size companies, I have exposed myself to various challenges involving user experience and visual design. My designs have been instrumental in making a difference in how people use applications.

To me, design is a problem-solving activity. As a designer, I understand that the perfect design not only involves visual aesthetics but even more importantly, an interface that functions well. This requires a deep understanding of human behavior and psychology - the outcome of which greatly impacts the overall experience of the product, regardless of the platform.

I personally enjoy working on projects that require both strategies as well as visual design. I love working on problems from the ideation stage and seeing them through fruition through pixel-perfect designs.
