Covid19 Mobile Application Design

A concept mobile application for users who would like to stay informed with current epidemic. An application that allow users to up-to-date with Covid19 news in their area, by providing real-time update on the amount of cases, keeping up with the news. It also helps users manage time during their trip as well as joining a community where they can share their travel experience
