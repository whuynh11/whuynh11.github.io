Synopsis

A brief small visual project for hotel booking. The idea is to create an app that would allow users to book a hotel of their choice with ease. Based on the initial research, this would be appeal to both business and pleasure occassion. The following features would create a dynamic ecosystem for better engagement: browsing & booking local experiences, chat function, ability to reschedule bookings, and rating & sharing the experience through social media.
