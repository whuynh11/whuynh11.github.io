Synopsis

The app is called, "Findy" which is short for Financial Independent app. My vision for this app is to sync user's bank accounts, credit cards, and paypal. Once a user link at least one bank account, they can start keeping track of incoming as well as outgoing money. In addition, users are able to keep track of their personal goals and save accordingly.
